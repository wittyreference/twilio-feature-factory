# Twilio Agent Factory - Design Decisions

This document captures architectural decisions for your Twilio application. Decisions may be revisited as you learn more - the changelog at the bottom tracks the evolution.

---

## How to Use This Document

Add a new decision entry when you make significant architectural choices. Each decision should capture:

1. **Context**: What problem or need drove this decision?
2. **Decision**: What did you decide?
3. **Rationale**: Why did you make this choice?
4. **Alternatives Considered**: What other options did you evaluate?
5. **Consequences**: What are the implications of this decision?
6. **Status**: Accepted, Superseded, or Under Review
